# Demo Video Script

SignalForge is an enterprise LLMOps control plane for managing AI quality, cost, latency, model routing, evaluations, and governance.

Start on the overview. The executive view shows monthly AI spend, P95 latency, quality score, and policy violations.

Move to Prompt Analytics. Teams can see which applications are driving requests, cost, cache hit rate, and latency.

Move to Evaluation Framework. This view compares model candidates across task-specific evals and shows individual failure modes.

Move to AI Routing. The platform routes requests based on complexity, risk, and cost-quality tradeoffs.

Move to Governance. This view shows policy health, blocked events, PII redactions, escalations, and audit actions.

End on Architecture. SignalForge creates the shared infrastructure layer enterprises need once AI usage scales beyond experiments.
