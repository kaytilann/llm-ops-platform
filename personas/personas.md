# Personas

## AI Platform PM
Needs a unified control plane for AI quality, cost, governance, and adoption.

## Platform Engineer
Needs reliable model gateway, routing, fallbacks, logging, and developer-friendly integration.

## Product Team Owner
Needs to launch AI features without becoming an expert in every model, eval, or policy requirement.

## Governance / Security Reviewer
Needs visibility into sensitive data exposure, policy violations, escalation logic, and audit trails.

## Finance Partner
Needs AI spend visibility, forecastable costs, and evidence that optimization efforts are working.
