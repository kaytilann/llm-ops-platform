# Product Strategy

## Product name
SignalForge AI Platform

## One-liner
An enterprise LLMOps control plane that helps AI teams manage quality, cost, latency, routing, evaluations, and governance across every AI application.

## Strategic thesis
As AI adoption scales, companies move from isolated chatbot experiments to a messy portfolio of AI-powered workflows. Without shared infrastructure, every team reinvents prompt management, evaluation, observability, cost controls, and governance.

SignalForge turns AI from scattered experiments into managed enterprise infrastructure.

## Target customer
Mid-market and enterprise companies running multiple internal or customer-facing AI applications.

## Primary buyers
- VP Engineering
- Head of AI Platform
- CIO / CTO
- Chief Data Officer
- Security and Governance leaders
- Product executives owning AI transformation

## Differentiation
Traditional observability platforms monitor infrastructure. SignalForge monitors AI decision quality, prompt behavior, model performance, and policy compliance.

## Strategic wedge
Start with prompt analytics and cost observability because spend pain is easy to quantify. Expand into evals, routing, governance, and automated optimization.
