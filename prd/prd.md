# PRD: SignalForge AI Platform

## Problem
Enterprise AI adoption is scaling faster than governance and infrastructure maturity. Teams are launching AI features without consistent controls for prompt quality, cost, latency, evals, model routing, or risk.

## Objective
Build a unified LLMOps control plane that gives teams visibility and control across AI applications.

## MVP scope

### Must have
- AI application inventory
- Prompt analytics
- Cost and latency dashboards
- Cache hit tracking
- Token breakdown
- Evaluation suite comparison
- Model promotion gate
- Routing rule configuration
- Governance event log
- Policy health scoring
- Architecture overview

## Success metrics
- Reduce AI spend by 20%
- Improve P95 latency by 30%
- Increase eval coverage to 80% of production AI workflows
- Reduce policy violations by 40%
- Achieve 90%+ model launch compliance with promotion gates
